Adam Silver

Adam Silver is an American Lawyer and Sports executive who is the fifth and current commissioner of the National Basketball Associaiton.

Silver was born on April 25, 1962 to a Jewish-American family in Rye, New York. He attended Duke University and was member of the Phi Delta Theta fraterninty, graduating in the year 1984 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in political science.

Before Silver became the commissioner, he worked as the NBA's deputy comminssioner and cheif operating officer for eight years. He was also apart of negociating the last three collective bargaining agreements withe the NBA players association, the development of the WNBA and the NBA development league, the partnership with Turner Broadcasting to manage the NBA's digital assets, and the creation of NBA China.

On October 25, 2012, he was endorsed by David Stern to be the next NBA commissioner. On February 1, 2014, when Stern stepped down from his position, Silver was unanimously approved by the NBA owners to succeed him.

Lebron James

Lebron Raymone James Sr. is an professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers.

Lebron was born on December 30, 1984 in Akron, Ohio and played for St. Vincent-St. Mary High School where he became heavily advertised for his size and ability as the next superstar and was even given the nickname "King James".

James was drafted as the first overall pick in the 2003 NBA draft by the Cleveland Cavaliers where he won rookie of the year with them.

James also played basketball for team USA for the first time coming off the bench at the 2004 Olympics in Athens, Greece where they finished with a bronze medal, becoming the first USA basketball team to finish without a gold medal since NBA players were allowed to compete. The last time he played for team USA was in the 2012 Olympics where he became the leader of the team and help lead them to a gold medal with the help of Kobe Bryant.

So far in his 21 year career, James has won four NBA championships, four NBA MVP awards, four NBA Finals MVP and is regarded by many as the greatest player of all time.

He is currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers where he won his last championship with in 2020

Micheal Jordan

Micheal Jeffery Jordan is an American buisnessman and former professional basketball player.

Jordan was born on Feburary 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York City.

When he finished high school in 1982, Jordan went to play basketball at the North Carolina Tar Heels under coach Dean Smith where he won a national championship with the team as a freshmen.

Two years later, Jordan would go to the NBA draft where he was selected by the Chicago Bulls with the number 3 pick. This would be the correct choice for chicago as Jordan became one of the leauges biggest stars with his scoring ability, defence and jumping capabilites which gave him the nickname "Air Jordan" after dunking from the free-throw line in a Slam Dunk Contest.

Throughout his 15 year career Jordan has won six NBA championships, five NBA MVP awards, five NBA finals MVP awards, two Olympic gold medals and has his number 23 retired with the Chicago Bulls and Miami Heat.

Stephen Curry

Wardell Stephen Curry II is the current point guard for the Golden State Warriors.

Curry was born on March 14, 1988 in Akron, Ohio to Sonya and Dell Curry who was a former NBA player for Clevland Cavaliers.

After highschool, Curry played college basketball for the Davidson Wildcats, where he helped the Wildcats advance to the elite eight in 2008.

His impressive two season at Davidson landed him as the seventh overall pick in the 2009 NBA draft. He was selected by the Golden State Warriors with the number 30 and has became one of the leauges greatest shooter and one of the greatest players of all time.

So far in his 15 year career Curry has 3 NBA championships with his last one being in 2022, 1 NBA finals MVP and 2 world cup gold medals
